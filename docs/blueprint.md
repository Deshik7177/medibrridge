# **App Name**: HealthSight

## Core Features:

- Data Collection: Collect community health data via integrated Google Forms.
- Data Analysis: Analyze collected health data to identify trends and correlations, and surface community-level reports for local clinics or NGOs.
- Risk Prediction: Predict individuals at high risk using regression models, to be used as a tool for prioritizing care.
- Interactive Dashboard: Display data in an interactive dashboard with charts showing disease trends and at-risk demographics.
- Automated Alerts: Send SMS/WhatsApp alerts to individuals identified as high risk.
- Freemium Reports: Offer free health reports to individuals and premium reports with advanced insights to NGOs or clinics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#2962FF) to convey trust, reliability, and health.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F7FA) to ensure a clean, modern, and accessible user interface.
- Accent color: Teal (#26A69A) for calls to action and highlighting key metrics.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for a modern and neutral feel.
- Use clean, simple icons that are easily understandable in the context of health data.
- Ensure a responsive layout that adapts to different devices (desktops, tablets, and smartphones) for accessibility in diverse community settings.
- Subtle transitions and animations to guide users through the data and provide feedback on interactions.
